About School

School name - SURAJMULL JAIN D.A.V PUBLIC SCHOOL

In the long and ever-expanding chain of DAV institutions this school was started on 1st July 1987, in the cherished memory of reverend Seth SURAJMULL JAIN under
the management of DAV Trust, New Delhi, with an objective of discovering and developing the special aptitude and hidden potentialities of the students of this tribal locality.
The school is running at Tata Road which is having the classes from LKG to Std XII. (Commerce and Science stream) and have all the modern equipments and facilities to match
the need of the students.
